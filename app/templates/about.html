About

Hi I am Shanna Williamson. I am currently an amateur photographer, but I aspire to become a proffesional photographer in the next few years. Photography for me has helped me understand my present, and resulted in me wanting to capture the moments in my life. Photography has made me look at life in a creative way, showing that art is all around us, it connotes the wonder and delight in the struggles and success, in the peace and chaos. As well as this, I have found that society sees the world through ugly eyes, but I wanted to show society in delightful eyes, yet still show the truth in our world. I want the moments I capture, to be memories you can keep forever. I have been studying photography throughout GCSE, achieving top grades, and then moving onto a higher level of education to enhace my photography skills.
